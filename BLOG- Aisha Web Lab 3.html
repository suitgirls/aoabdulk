Mary Louise Prather Journey

Aisha Abdulkadir / july 22nd, 2025

Mary Louise Prather was a world war 2 code breaker for the U.S Army.she learned to read languages like germany and japanese just to read secret messages and spent hours finding patterns in radio traffic. Because of her hard work,commanders knew where and when the enemy troops and supplies were,which led to svaing many peoples lives. After working there as a level entry worker she was later promoted to admininstritive assisstant and personal officer. But this accomplishment was a big deal because not many women of her time never had such opportunity. she in the workforce around world war 2 in the 1940's. Mary Louise Prather showed that women can do amazing things even in a feild job as difficuly as the military.
